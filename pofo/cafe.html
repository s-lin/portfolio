Q² Bakery & Café

visual identity, interior, and furniture design for concept bakery café, Q²

Q² is a bakery/café concept born out of a love for a particular texture- q. As someone who cannot smell, the texture of food holds great importance for me. The bakery itself is designed around the physical feeling of eating and hopes to communicate the essence of the texture "q" to its customers
Primary user/clientel of the café
Software used: SketchupPro, Adobe Illustrator
